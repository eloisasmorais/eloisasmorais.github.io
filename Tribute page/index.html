Hayley Williams

Hayley Williams is the lead singer and songwriter of an American band called Paramore.
Williams has been a professional singer since 2003, when she was 14 years old. Alongside her bandmates, she released four studio albums and two live albums, they also featured in movie soundtracks. Besides her successful carreer as a singer, Hayley also has a hair dye company called Good Dye Young.

Albums

  • All We Know Is Falling (2005)
  • Riot! (2007)
  • Brand New Eyes (2009)
  • Paramore(2013)
  • After Laughter (2017)

Awards

  • Best Rock Song - Grammy Awards (2015)
  • Best Vocalist - Alternative Press Music Awards (2015)
  • Trailblazer Award - Billboard Women in Music (2014)
  • Hook Up Song - Teen Choice Award (2010)
  • Best International Act - Video Music Brasil (2008)
